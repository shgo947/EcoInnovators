Find the Nearest E-Waste Recycling Center

Dispose of your electronic waste responsibly by using our E-Waste Facility Locator. We make it easy for you to find the nearest recycling centers and drop-off points for your old electronic devices.

Why Recycle E-Waste?

Electronic waste, or e-waste, can be harmful to the environment if not properly disposed of. Many electronic devices contain hazardous materials that can leach into the soil and water if left in landfills. Recycling e-waste helps reduce environmental pollution and conserves valuable resources.

How to Use the Locator

Using our E-Waste Facility Locator is simple:

  1. Enter your location or ZIP code in the search bar.
  2. Click the "Search" button to view a list of nearby recycling centers.
  3. Find the center that is most convenient for you and follow the directions to drop off your e-waste.
